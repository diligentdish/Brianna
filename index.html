Annual Theme Identity System

Context

The client required a unified visual theme derived from a specific scripture and phrase to anchor its annual programming and community outreach.

Creative Approach

  • Developed a typography-forward composition to emphasize the key phrase
  • Selected color palette based on emotional tone of the scripture
  • Designed layout for scalability across vertical banners and horizontal garment applications
  • Balanced legibility with expressive hierarchy

Tools Used

  • Canva
  • ProCreate

Results

Design adopted across multiple physical and digital touchpoints to establish a consistent visual anchor for all related communications

Weekly Announcements QR System

Context

The church requested a clearer, budget-friendly way to communicate weekly announcements, events, and service information. Traditional handouts and static flyers were costly to reproduce and quickly became outdated, creating the need for a more flexible, centralized communication method.

Creative Approach

  • Designed a "flyer of flyers" concept using a single QR code hub for all announcements
  • Created a clear, high-contrast layout for easy scanning from a distance
  • Designed and organized the linked announcement website with consistent visual cards for each service and event
  • Ensured the system could be updated weekly without reprinting, using a streamlined process that could be easily taught and maintained by any team member

Tools Used

  • Canva
  • Canva Website Builder
  • QR Code Generator

Results

Implemented a centralized announcement system that allowed members and guests to quickly access services, ministries, and upcoming events from one scan. Reduced the need for printed handouts and created a reusable, scalable communication solution for weekly updates

Radio Station Branding

Context

Local radio station focused on community news, interviews, and live discussions wanted a rebrand. The goal was to create a strong, recognizable identity that felt modern, energetic, and suitable for both digital and broadcast use.

Creative Approach

  • Developed a bold, typographic logo centered around "HD" to create an instantly recognizable brand mark for a High Desert radio station
  • Integrated a microphone element to visually communicate the live radio and interview-based format
  • Added a subtle metallic and lighting effect to give the logo a polished, professional, on-air feel
  • Built the logo as a flexible system that could scale across social media graphics, broadcast visuals, and merchandise

Tools Used

  • Adobe Illustrator (primary design & vector logo creation)
  • Adobe Creative Cloud

Results

Delivered a clean, adaptable logo that could be used across digital platforms, promotional materials, and broadcast graphics, giving the program a consistent and professional identity.
